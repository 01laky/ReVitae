Why bundled: ReVitae renders CV PDFs with QuestPDF. Referencing a host-installed
"Arial" made rendering depend on the machine's fonts, so the same CV exported
differently on Windows/macOS/Linux. Embedding Arimo (which shares Arial's
metrics) makes export byte-identical on every platform.
